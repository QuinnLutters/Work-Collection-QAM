Research report

If you do any research it is also important to put all the information you’ve gathered into a report, since this allows you to have a place where you can go back to see what you’ve done and how this can help you. Before I started to make my research report I did some research into research reports, since I didn’t know where to start. For this research I used literature study, since I wanted to do find some studies where they talked about how to make one and what you can put into it. I actually found something really useful from an American university which gave me an example on how I could make a research report.
