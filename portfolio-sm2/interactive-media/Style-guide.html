Creating a style guide

Designers can have a lot of trouble when they make any product for a client, because the client can use for example a logo in any way they want to, but that can destroy the entire logo. That’s why it is important to make a style guide, since a style guide allows you to guide your client or even yourself on how to use any product that you make. It can also help making the design of website and or apps more consistent, since you can guide people in which font, color, margins, etc. to use. That’s also the reason why I made my style guide. I started of by doing some research on what I had to include in a style guide and I also needed some inspiration. I decide to include which colors I was going to use, how you should use my logo and which typefaces are allowed to be used. After some sketching I came up with some loose layouts and started to work on my style guide and it’s content in InDesign. While making the pages I went to multiple iterations until I found something that I liked and also worked, where everything had enough white space.
