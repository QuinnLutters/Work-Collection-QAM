Brand extensions

For my brand extension The candle I had to off course make a design of a candle and I also decided to make a web page. I started of by creating an paper prototype for my candle and I wanted to use the actual toys Fenia made, to represent the candles. So I started by drawing the toy and from there on I decided to put in a class casing, with the candle inside of it. The glass casing has a wooden bottom, the lid will be made of the same wood. The actual candle itself won’t be in the exact shape of the toy, but it will have the rough outline of the toy and the actual print itself will be printed onto the glass. The candle will have a scent, a Greek coast scent.

As for the webpage I wanted it to have the same feel as the actual website we’re creating, so I used the same navigation and layout. I wanted to divide the page into three different sections, first the introduction to the candle, to showcase it, then the “product page” where you can view all the images, see the price and order the candle. The product information which in this case are just the materials. I wanted the materials to have a description and I decided to add that by adding an information icon and making the entire material click able, which makes a window pop up with the material description.

After doing testing I got some feedback on what could maybe be better on the website and I therefore made a bar underneath the image at the product page, which displayed all images and arrows to click through the images, because the users didn’t like the fact that you had to click on the image in order to be able to click through all the images.
