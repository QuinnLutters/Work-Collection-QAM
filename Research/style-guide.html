Style guide

For my style guide I’ve also done some research since I had no clue on how to make one and what to put into it, and off course some examples for some inspiration won’t hurt. For this research I used literature study to do some web research and go through some presentations from my teachers to find out what I can put into my style guide. I found that style guides are really useful since it give you and your clients a guideline or manual on how to use any of your products, so your products won’t get ruined.

After again doing some web research on this topic I could have used different CMD methods to improve my end result, since you need a lot of examples for this to know where to start of with. Comparison chart would have been a great help, since it would have allowed me to grab different examples and compare them with each other to see what everyone is doing and what everyone is not doing. This would have helped me to get a general better understanding of this topic and therefore improved my end product.
